Efficiency calculation is the # of big blinds won per hundred hands. The big blind is always assumed to be 50 cents. To be on the leader board you need to play a minimum # of hands and be in the positive for winnings.. The minimum # of hands is the # week in the year * 150 for any # of players at table. For a specifc range of players at the table it is the # week in the year * 40.
