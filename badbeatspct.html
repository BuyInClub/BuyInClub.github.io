Some information on how wretched hands are calculated and the information provided:

  • A wretched hand can happen on any street
  • Only 2 players can be in at the end of the hand
  • The threshold to get a wretched hand is calculated as 2*(19 percent)/# num players in the hand
  • Percentage of ties is added to the percent of winning
  • Cards shown by other players at end of hand are used in the calculation of percent to win, they are considered dead cards
  • On the street the WH threshold is met, a minimum of $10 must be in the pot
  • The percent column will have alway have the lowest percent the WH winner had to win
  • Any amounts in columns starting with 'Pot' will be based on the entire hand
  • Any amounts in columns starting with 'WH' will be based on the end of the last street the WH winner was under the threshold
