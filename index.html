Welcome to the Buyin Club

This site is to provide us with some fun statistics and hand details from our poker nights. This is a work in progress. This initial page will provide a summary of the different sessions and overall player totals. From this page you can click into the last session and start exploring. Due to space limitations on the provider github only the last poker night will have a link. Enjoy Tyrell.
